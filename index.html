About This Project

This project was created as a result of my decision to start postgraduate studies in "Cybersecurity Engineering" at the Warsaw University of Technology, where I am deepening my knowledge in the field of cybersecurity. The goal of the project is to develop skills related to ethical hacking, penetration testing, and modern security practices, which will allow me to effectively counter digital threats.

On this site, I will be publishing the results of my tests as well as code that will be useful in the field of cybersecurity. These materials will be available to anyone who wants to expand their knowledge in this area and for those looking for tools to test and improve the security of their systems.
